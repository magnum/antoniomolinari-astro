Hi, I'm **Antonio Molinari** — Magnum to friends. Developer and designer, I
live near Cremona in northern Italy with my wife and two kids. I've been
working on the web since the late 90s, so I've seen quite a few things come and
go. With my studio **Incode** I handle web development projects: mainly
**Ruby/Rails** (an almost irrational attachment, I'll admit), open source CMS,
Node.js and frontend development with Vue, AI integration, and everything in
between — from infrastructure to UX. I've also served as **CTO** and **mentor**
for several startups, on projects ranging from blockchain-based asset tracking
to document management. Since 2013 I've been teaching web design at **LABA —
Libera Accademia di Belle Arti** in Brescia, which keeps me in constant contact
with people approaching technology from a creative rather than a technical
background. Passionate about electronic music, live concerts, and anything with
a chip, a network connection, or something worth taking apart to understand how
it works.
